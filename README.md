# Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to understand overall business performance, identify key revenue drivers, and uncover insights that support data-driven decision-making. The analysis covers sales trends, product and category performance, regional contributions, and profitability using SQL and Power BI.

The final output is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed for business stakeholders.

## Business Objectives
- Analyze how sales and profit have changed over time
- Identify top-performing products, categories, and regions
- Understand seasonal sales patterns
- Evaluate the relationship between sales and profitability
- Highlight areas for potential business improvement

## Tools & Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning and preparation
- **SQL (SQLite)** – Data analysis and querying
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and interactive dashboard
- **GitHub** – Project version control and portfolio hosting

## Dataset
- Public Superstore Sales Dataset downloaded from Kaggle
- Cleaned and prepared for analysis
- Saved as: `sales_cleaned.csv`

## Analysis Approach
1. Defined key business questions before analysis
2. Cleaned and structured raw sales data in Excel
3. Performed sales, profit, and regional analysis using SQL
4. Validated results through exploratory data analysis
5. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize insights

## Key Business Insights
- Total sales reached **$2.3M**, with an overall profit margin of **12.47%**
- Sales show steady growth over time with clear seasonal peaks toward year-end
- The **West region** is the strongest contributor to revenue, followed by the East  
- **Technology** is the highest-performing category in terms of sales
- Several high-selling products generate low or negative profit, indicating pricing or cost issues
- High revenue does not always translate into high profitability, highlighting the need for profit-focused decision-making

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, and Total Orders  
- Sales trend analysis over time
- Sales performance by region and category
- Top products by sales and profit
- Interactive filters for Date, Region, and Category

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates a complete end-to-end data analysis workflow, combining technical skills with business-focused insights. It reflects real-world tasks performed by junior data analysts and emphasizes translating data into actionable business understanding.
